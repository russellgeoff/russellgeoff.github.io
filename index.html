Bio

Geoff Russell is an medical device engineer located in San Jose, California. Geoff's specialities include mechanical design and computer vision applications. Geoff has experience creating products for the orthopedic and general surgery market in America and the obstetric market in developing nations. Geoff Russell has created reimbursement, regulatory, and business plans for these devices along with the actual design.
